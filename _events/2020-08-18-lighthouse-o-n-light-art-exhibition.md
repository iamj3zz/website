description: "The exhibition presents works by artists of Lighthouse light art society at an unusual location utilizing the theater's distinctive atmosphere as an improvised gallery. It features analogue and digital light art pieces alongside kinetic installations exploring manmade structures, reflective surfaces and light as a medium. The artworks examine constant change and variable permanence through various light forms. Participating Artists: Zalán Adorján, Gáspár Battha, László Zsolt Bordos, Healium collective, Tamás Herczeg, Kiégő Izzók, Gábor Kitzinger, Ivó Kovács, Jérome LI THIAO TÉ, Rozi Mákó, Erik Mátrai, Martin Mikolai, András Nagy, G Csongor Szigeti, Csilla Szilágyi, Hanna Tardos, Viktor Vicsek/Limelight.
