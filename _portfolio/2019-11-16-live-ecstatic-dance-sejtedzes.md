  Ecstatic Dance + Sejtedzés Matisz Vikivel was a monthly Ecstatic Dance Budapest event co-organized by Watcha Studios, held at MagNet Közösségi Ház in Budapest. The evening opened with a "Sejtedzés" movement workshop led by Viki Matisz, followed by a four-hour ecstatic dance session on a 230 m² dance floor. J3ZZ performed as a live violinist alongside DJ Konrad and fellow live performers Fatoumata Hari and Simon Gyuri, in an alcohol- and drug-free open space for free movement. The event drew 350 Facebook responses.
