description: |
  J3ZZ participated as violinist in events organised by Zene a Rasszizmus Ellen (ZARE — Music Against Racism), a Hungarian nonprofit collective working towards a sustainable society through intercultural dialogue and the power of music.

  ZARE is Hungary's adaptation of the British Rock Against Racism and Love Music Hate Racism movements, established in 2009 with support from the Budapest British Embassy and coordinated by the Szubjektív Értékek Alapítvány (Subjective Values Foundation). The organisation uses music as a medium to advocate for human equality regardless of political sympathy.

  J3ZZ performed at recurring Anti-Racism World Day (Antirasszista Világnap) events at Budapest's Millenáris Park cultural complex, and at the Daniel Pearl World Music Days concert at Bazaar Klub — an international music network celebrating tolerance through live performance.
