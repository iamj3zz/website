  Guest appearance as violinist with Rebel Star, an alternative country/pop rock band formed in 2006 by Milan Glavaški (former Eva Braun and Popcycle member) with musicians from Bečej, Serbia and Szentendre, Hungary. The band had released three studio albums by this date — *So* (2007), *Kalifonija* (2009), and *Rebel Star* (2012) — on labels including Label Star, Odličan Hrčak, and Dancing Bear, and were promoting their fourth album *Reka* (released 2015). Performed live at Dom omladine Beograda (DOB // Klub), Belgrade, on October 11, 2014.
