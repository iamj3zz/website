- type: text
  title: Video Installation as Artistic Practice
  content: |
    **IN BETWEEN** represents work at the intersection of video installation, short film, and sound art — disciplines that were increasingly converging in the early 2010s. The project demonstrates Massih Parsaei's commitment to creating immersive multimedia experiences that engage viewers on multiple sensory levels.

    As a video installation, the work moves beyond traditional cinema — it occupies a space, demands physical presence, and creates an environment rather than simply telling a story. The format invites viewers into an aesthetic and conceptual space, where image, sound, and presence interact dynamically.
- type: text
  title: Sonic Dimension
  content: |
    J3ZZ's compositional work for **IN BETWEEN** was integral to the piece's overall impact. The original soundtrack was designed not as musical accompaniment but as a co-equal element — a sonic architecture that supports, complicates, and deepens the visual experience.

    The collaboration between visual artist and sound composer represents a fundamental creative principle: that meaningful art emerges from the dialogue between disciplines, and that the strongest works engage multiple senses simultaneously.
