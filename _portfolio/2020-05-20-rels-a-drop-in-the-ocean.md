  A Drop in the Ocean is a solo album by J3ZZ, released on May 20, 2020. Three long-form tracks — Erosion, Downdrift, and High Tide — each running between ten and fifteen minutes. The album was performed live using synths, sequencers, violin, and effects pedals, with no post-production editing to the performances themselves.

  The ocean metaphor runs through every layer of the record: slow, immersive movements that build and recede like tides. The tracks do not rush. They erode, drift, and surge on their own terms — inviting the listener to surrender to the pace rather than control it.

    text_content: |
      *A Drop in the Ocean* is a **solo album** by J3ZZ — three long-form live performances for synths, sequencers, violin, and effects pedals. Each track is a single sustained journey: *Erosion*, *Downdrift*, *High Tide*.

      The ocean is not a backdrop here — it is the structure. Erosion works slowly, wearing down resistance until only texture remains. Downdrift surrenders to current, letting sequences and harmonics pull the music somewhere unplanned. High Tide arrives with accumulated force, the inevitable culmination of everything that built beneath the surface.

      Together the three tracks form a meditative arc — patient, physical, unhurried. The album rewards those willing to drift with it.
